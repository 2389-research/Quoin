A small follow-up to 1.1.0: the outline now follows your reading position.

### Added
- **The outline follows along.** In a long document, the outline inspector keeps the section you're currently reading in view — scrolling to it as you cross into a new section. It moves the minimum needed (a section already on screen stays put), never fights you when you scroll or collapse the outline yourself, and is instant under Reduce Motion.
